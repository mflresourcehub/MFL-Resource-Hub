Discover CLIL!

Content and Language Integrated Learning (CLIL) is an educational approach that combines language learning with subject-specific content. Instead of teaching a language as a separate subject, CLIL integrates it into lessons within other curricular areas such as social subjects and sciences, allowing students to develop both their language skills and subject knowledge simultaneously. This immersive method helps learners acquire language skills naturally while engaging with meaningful content. CLIL encourages active learning, critical thinking, and communication skills, making it an effective way to enhance both subject knowledge and linguistic competence.

Find out more about how to implement CLIL in your classroom and browse our range of CLIL resources.
